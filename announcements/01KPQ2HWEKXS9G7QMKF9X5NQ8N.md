Hello, beta testers!

This is the first live announcement fed through the new in-app system.
Your instance polled [Dictionarry-Hub/bulletin](https://github.com/Dictionarry-Hub/bulletin),
found this entry, and surfaced it in your sidebar.

This system exists so breaking changes, migrations, and deprecations
reach every Profilarr instance in-app instead of only landing on Discord
or GitHub.

A few pointers while you're here:

- Expand a row to read the body.
- Click the **eye icon** in the actions column to mark an announcement
  read (or unread, if you change your mind). The **count pill** on the
  Announcements nav item reflects unread items and clears as you mark
  them.
- Expired or withdrawn items quietly disappear on the next fetch.

Designs live in the [backend docs](https://github.com/Dictionarry-Hub/profilarr/blob/develop/docs/backend/announcements.md)
if you'd like to understand how it works under the hood.

Thanks for testing v2.
