Hello, beta testers!

A new feature called Drift just shipped. It helps you track whether your
Arr instance is still in sync with what Profilarr would push today. Each
connected Arr now has a Drift tab that compares the live config against
your Profilarr setup and lists anything that's drifted, with the exact
differences side by side.

This field level diff tracking also enables progress markers on the Sync
tab, which never quite worked properly in v1. Once drift has run, each
section surfaces a small progress indicator showing how much of that section is still in
sync, all driven by the same data. You don't have to do anything beyond
enabling drift on the instance to see them.

A few practical things:

- Drift is off by default. Open the Drift tab on any Arr instance to
  turn it on and pick a schedule.
- It's strictly observational. Profilarr never writes back to Arr from
  a drift check.
- It fires a notification the first time it spots something new, then
  stays quiet until that changes.
